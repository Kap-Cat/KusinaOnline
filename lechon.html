Lechon Recipe

Author: John Doe Date Published: December 2, 2024

Classic Filipino Lechon

Enjoy the crispy, juicy, and flavorful goodness of Filipino lechon! Perfect for large celebrations and festive gatherings.

Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 5 hours
Total Time: 5 hours 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 whole pig (about 10-15 kg)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup calamansi or lemon juice
  • 8 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2-3 stalks lemongrass (tied into a knot)
  • 1 large onion (quarter cut)
  • 1 bunch green onions
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Clean the pig and remove excess fat. Pat dry with paper towels.
  2. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, calamansi juice, garlic, sugar, black pepper, and salt to make the marinade.
  3. Rub the marinade all over the pig, including the inside. Stuff the pig with lemongrass, onion, and green onions.
  4. Allow the pig to marinate for at least 3-4 hours, preferably overnight.
  5. Roast on a large spit over a charcoal grill. Turn regularly for even cooking.
  6. Roast for 4-5 hours or until skin is golden brown and crispy.
  7. Let it rest before carving and serving.

Fun Facts About Lechon

  • Served during major celebrations like Christmas and birthdays.
  • Roasted whole on a spit until crispy.
  • Symbol of hospitality and abundance.

Nutrition Information (Per Serving)

Calories: 450 | Protein: 35g | Fat: 30g | Carbs: 10g
