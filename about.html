Our Journey

Kusina Online started as a small community of passionate home cooks and food enthusiasts eager to share recipes that celebrate Filipino heritage. Our vision was to create an online space where everyone, from beginners to culinary experts, could explore, share, and preserve our rich culinary traditions while embracing modern innovations.
