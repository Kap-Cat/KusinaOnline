Halo-Halo Recipe

Author: John Doe Date Published: December 2, 2024

Classic Filipino Halo-Halo

A refreshing and colorful dessert that is perfect for hot days, combining shaved ice with a variety of sweet ingredients!

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 0 minutes
Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Shaved ice
  • Sweetened red beans
  • Sweetened jackfruit
  • Sweetened banana (saba variety)
  • Leche flan (caramelized egg custard)
  • Ube (purple yam) ice cream
  • Coconut strips
  • Sweetened macapuno (coconut sport)
  • Evaporated milk

Instructions

  1. In a tall glass, layer the sweetened beans, jackfruit, banana, and coconut strips.
  2. Top with shaved ice, making sure it reaches the top of the glass.
  3. Add a few scoops of ube ice cream and leche flan on top of the shaved ice.
  4. Drizzle with evaporated milk to taste.
  5. Mix everything together and enjoy!

Fun Facts About Halo-Halo

  • Halo-Halo is a Filipino dessert that means "mix-mix" in English, which reflects the way the ingredients are mixed together.
  • It is especially popular during the hot summer months in the Philippines.
  • The dessert is made with a variety of ingredients, and no two versions are exactly the same!
