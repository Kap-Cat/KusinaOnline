Chicken Adobo Recipe

Author: John Doe Date Published: December 2, 2024

Classic Filipino Chicken Adobo

A savory and tangy dish that embodies the heart of Filipino cuisine. Perfect for family dinners and celebrations!

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 45 minutes
Total Time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, peppercorns, and bay leaves. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add water and bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer for 30 minutes, or until chicken is tender.
  4. Optional: Add sugar to balance the tanginess, if desired.
  5. Serve with steamed rice and enjoy!

Fun Facts About Adobo

  • Adobo is considered the unofficial national dish of the Philippines.
  • The dish can be made with chicken, pork, or even beef.
  • It is believed that the name "adobo" comes from the Spanish word "adobar," which means "to marinate."
